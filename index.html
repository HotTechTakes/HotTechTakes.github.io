About

Hot Tech Takes (HTT) is associated with the InfoTrust Foundation, a 501(c)(3) non-profit here in the Cincinnati area. The mission of the InfoTrust Foundation is to give back to communities in which our team members live and work. As a nonprofit organization, we have donated labor and money to organizations around to world to better education for all students, help fight poverty, and provide healthcare.
